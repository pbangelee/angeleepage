My Hobbies

Photography

Photography has always been a gateway to the world's diverse landscapes and cultures for me. I specialize in nature and event photography, seeking to find the untold stories behind each subject I capture.

Videography

From scripting to post-production, I love every aspect of videography. I’ve produced short films and documentaries that explore human emotion and resilience, presented through a cinematic lens.

Dancing

The vibrant rhythms and expressive movements of Polynesian dance have always resonated with me. As a performer, the connection to heritage and the joy of sharing it through dance is profoundly rewarding. Polynesian dance isn't just a hobby—it's a celebration of culture that I hold close to my heart.

Graphic Design

Graphic design combines my love for art and technology. I enjoy experimenting with color, typography, and layouts to produce designs that are not only visually appealing but also effective in communicating a clear message.

Travel and Exploration

Adventure Travel

From scaling mountains to diving into the depths of the ocean, adventure travel challenges me and pushes the boundaries of my comfort zone. Each journey is a testament to the beauty and diversity of our planet.

Greenery & Gardening

Gardening offers a sanctuary where I can engage with nature even in the midst of an urban landscape. There's something deeply gratifying about the simplicity of soil between your fingers, the patience of nurturing seedlings, and the reward of blooms that color the seasons.
